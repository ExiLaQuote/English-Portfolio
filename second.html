[II]: PASSIONATE INTELLECT
⇣


First, intellect will only go as far as my passion and determination are willing to take it. That is the principle I have discovered through all my years of learning. I will never claim to be smart, because the true underlying virtue is the drive and heart to educate myself in everything. After studying English for school, I would study linguistics at home. To take a break from physics, I rest by watching videos on quantum mechanics.


Regardless of how it sounds, my motivation and disposition are no different from that of one who likes to watch baking vlogs. However, sometimes my love for a subject is not equivalent to my competence. Such was the case for Filipino, singing, mathematics, and science. I was helplessly rubbish at them, and I realized that it takes more love to be good at something I am not. So I chose to persist through midnights, summers, and Christmas breaks.

The walls on those floors were casted with iron.
